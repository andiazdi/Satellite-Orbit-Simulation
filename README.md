## You may edit variables to change planet or to see the diffrence 

>G = 6.673 * 10 ** (-11)

>M = 5.972 * 10 ** 24

>radius = 6371000

>ISS_height = 437 * 10 ** 3

>ISS_velocity = 8000

>ISS_time = 90 * 60
